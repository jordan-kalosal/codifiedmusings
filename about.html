About my blog

Hey there! Welcome to my personal blog: Codified Musings! This site is a compilation of my thoughts (or musings) on a variety of topics. As you can probably tell by the categories above, I divide my work into three main groups: Economics, Leadership and Web Development. I write to teach, inspire and - perhaps more than anything else - codify my own understanding of complex topics. To get a better grasp of the purpose of this blog, check out my very first blog post: Hello, world.

About me

Though seemingly disparate, my passions lie in web development, user experience design and education. To satiate my interests, I manage a small web development company - Kalosal Solutions - which builds web applications and mobile apps with a variety of applications. From building fundraising platforms for NGO's to crafting ubiquitous mobile apps in the field of education technology, we have a blast creating usable solutions to otherwise formidable challenges.

More recently, I've become a cofounder of EditRevise - a feedback on demand service that currently provides web applications to connect freelance editors with content writers across the world.

On the personal front, I'm a huge fan of both Twenty One Pilots and apple juice. I believe that the best measure of value in an experience is how different it is from all other experiences one has. As such, I enjoy learning just about anything from just about anyone.
